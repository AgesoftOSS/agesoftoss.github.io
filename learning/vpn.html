What is a VPN?

VPN („Virtual Private Network“) is a service to browse in the web with a different IP Address which is difficult to backtrack. VPNs are commonly used to be hidden on the web and to bypass internet cencoreships.

How does a VPN work

Normal Internet-Traffic aren't encrypted and public. By connecting to a VPN your IP address get's hidden and you get a new IP address which is the server you selected to connect.
