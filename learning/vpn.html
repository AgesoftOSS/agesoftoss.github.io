What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) allows users to browse the web with a different IP address, making it harder to trace their online activities. It works by encrypting internet traffic and routing it through a server, which replaces the user's IP address with the server's IP address. This helps to enhance privacy and bypass internet censorship.

How does a VPN work

Normal Internet-Traffic aren't encrypted and public. By connecting to a VPN your IP address get's hidden and you get a new IP address which is the server you selected to connect.
