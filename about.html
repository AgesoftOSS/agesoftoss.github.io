What exactly is Agesoft?

Agesoft is a munich based german IT Company owned by Entertainmasters and founded by Copy05

What do we do?

We develop products, solutions and Developer Tools to help people in various fields especially in the IT Industry. We want to work independent from other companies and people for 100% productivity and freedom

Our History:

It all started back in 2016 where a german student called Copy05 found interests in programming he first started with Visual Basic.NET. Later in 2019 Agesoft has been founded and acquired by Entertainmasters as their IT Subsidiary. After the acquirement Agesoft shifted into C++ / Javascript & Java Programming instead of VB.NET Programming In 2021 Agesoft started focusing on Open Source Project (OSS) and releaced projects like: Consoleutilities and AWebJS
