AntiCopyright

Protect your copyrighted works by detecting and removing these from search engines like Google

Why you should choose AntiCopyright

Independentcy is important and protecting copyrighted content is important too. With AntiCopyright you can Track, Monitor and Research search results of search engines. all found entries will be logged locally on your computer for a better overview

Pro and Cons:

Pro:
- Lightweight
- Free
- Open-Source

Cons:
- Limmited to Google
- Chance for getting Rate Limmited
- Requires Python with these requirements
- No Linux Support for Logging
